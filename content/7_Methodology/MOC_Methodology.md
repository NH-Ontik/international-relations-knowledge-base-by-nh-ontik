## Decision Tree: Which Method?

```
Research Question
├── How / Why? (single or few cases)
│   ├── Causal mechanism? → Process Tracing (qualitative)
│   ├── Cross-case comparison? → QCA or Comparative Case Studies
│   └── Meaning / interpretation? → Discourse Analysis, Ethnography
├── How much / how many? (many cases)
│   ├── Observational data? → [[Quantitative Methods in IR]]
│   ├── Natural experiment? → Natural Experiments
│   └── Text data? → Text-as-Data, Machine Learning
└── What if? (formal reasoning)
    ├── Strategic interaction? → Game Theory Models
    └── Emergent dynamics? → [[Formal Modeling and Agent-Based Modeling]]
```
