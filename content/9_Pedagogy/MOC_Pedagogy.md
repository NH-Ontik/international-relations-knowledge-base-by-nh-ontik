| Resource | Description | Use Case |

## How to Use These Notes

- **Starting a course?** Begin with [[Great Debates in IR]] for intellectual context, then consult [[IR Glossary]] as needed.
- **Writing an essay?** Check [[Citation Guide for IR]] for formatting; reference [[Great Debates in IR]] for theoretical framing.
- **Choosing a dissertation topic?** [[State of the Field 2025-2026]] identifies current gaps and debates.
- **Revision?** The glossary is a rapid-fire test-yourself tool; the debates note provides the spine of IR intellectual history.
