# US Foreign Policy

## Overview
The world's most powerful state and central case study across all IR theory. US foreign policy is the primary reference for realism (hegemony, unipolarity), liberalism (institutional leadership), Marxism (imperialism), constructivism (identity/exceptionalism), and FPA (presidential decision-making).

## Key Themes
- Unipolar moment (1991–present decline?)
- Exceptionalism as national identity
- Democracy promotion vs realist restraint
- Interventionism vs isolationism
- Alliance leadership (NATO, bilateral alliances)

1. Is the US a declining hegemon?
2. Does US FP prioritise democracy or strategic interests?
3. What explains continuity vs change in US grand strategy?
