# Bangladesh Foreign Policy

## Overview
A small state in a complex region, Bangladesh's foreign policy is shaped by geography, economic development needs, and a liberation-war founding identity. Central case for small state diplomacy and comparative FPA.

## Key Themes
- Small state constraints
- Balancing between India and China
- Economic diplomacy and development-driven FP
- Rohingya crisis and humanitarian leadership
- Multilateral engagement (UN peacekeeping contributions)
- Climate vulnerability as foreign policy driver
