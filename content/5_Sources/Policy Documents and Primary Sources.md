## United Nations

### UN Charter (1945)
- **Adopted:** 26 June 1945, San Francisco
- **Full Text:** [UN.org](https://www.un.org/en/about-us/un-charter/full-text)
- **Treaty Collection:** [treaties.un.org](https://treaties.un.org/PAGES/ViewDetails.aspx?src=TREATY&mtdsg_no=I-1&chapter=1&clang=_en)
- **PDF:** [UN Treaty Collection](https://treaties.un.org/doc/publication/ctc/uncharter.pdf)

### Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR, 1948)
- **Adopted:** 10 December 1948, Paris (GA Res. 217 A)
- **Full Text:** [UN.org](https://www.un.org/en/about-us/universal-declaration-of-human-rights)
- **OHCHR Translations:** [ohchr.org](https://www.ohchr.org/en/human-rights/universal-declaration/translations/english)
- **PDF:** [UN.org](https://www.un.org/sites/un2.un.org/files/2021/03/udhr.pdf)

### Vienna Convention on the Law of Treaties (1969)
- **Adopted:** 23 May 1969, Vienna; entered into force 27 January 1980
- **Full Text:** [treaties.un.org](https://treaties.un.org/pages/ViewDetails.aspx?chapter=23&clang=_en&mtdsg_no=XXIII-1&src=TREATY)
- **PDF:** [UN International Law Commission](https://legal.un.org/ilc/texts/instruments/english/conventions/1_1_1969.pdf)

### Rome Statute of the International Criminal Court (1998)
- **Adopted:** 17 July 1998, Rome; entered into force 1 July 2002
- **Full Text:** [treaties.un.org](https://treaties.un.org/pages/ViewDetails.aspx?chapter=18&clang=_en&mtdsg_no=XVIII-10&src=TREATY)
- **ICC PDF:** [icc-cpi.int](https://www.icc-cpi.int/sites/default/files/2025-05/Rome-Statute-EN-2025.pdf)
- **UN PDF:** [legal.un.org](https://legal.un.org/icc/statute/english/rome_statute(e).pdf)
