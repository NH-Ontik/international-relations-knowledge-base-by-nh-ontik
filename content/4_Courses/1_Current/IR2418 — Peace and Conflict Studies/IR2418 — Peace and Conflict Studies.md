## Course Structure

1. **Foundations** (Module 1): Negative/positive peace, peace research history, key debates
2. **Violence Triangle** (Module 2): Direct, structural, cultural violence — Galtung's framework
3. **Conflict Analysis** (Module 3): ABC triangle, root causes, needs, escalation models
4. **Conflict Transformation** (Module 4): Lederach's four dimensions, peacebuilding pyramid
5. **Peacebuilding** (Module 5): Liberal peace, local turn, hybrid peace, sustaining peace
6. **Track Diplomacy** (Module 6): Track I/II, mediation, ripeness, Multi-Track
7. **Peacekeeping** (Module 7): 3 generations, Brahimi/HIPPO/A4P, Bangladesh as TCC
8. **Non-Western Approaches** (Module 8): Satyagraha, Ubuntu, Sulh, feminist, postcolonial
9. **Transitional Justice** (Module 9): Four pillars, peace vs justice, reconciliation
10. **Contemporary Challenges** (Module 10): Climate, technology, WPS, authoritarian peace, synthesis
