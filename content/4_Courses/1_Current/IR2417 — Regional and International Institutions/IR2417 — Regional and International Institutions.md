## Course Structure

1. **Theoretical Foundations** (Module 1): What are IOs? IR theories applied to IOs
2. **The UN System** (Modules 2–3): Principal organs, specialised agencies, reform
3. **Regional IOs** (Module 4): EU, AU, ASEAN, SAARC, and others
4. **Bretton Woods Institutions** (Modules 5–7): IMF, World Bank, WTO
5. **Security Institutions** (Module 8): NATO, SCO, collective security vs collective defence
6. **R2P and Intervention** (Module 9): Humanitarian intervention, R2P doctrine
7. **Global Governance Challenges** (Module 10): Climate, health, digital
8. **New Institutions** (Module 11): BRICS+, AIIB, NDB, G20, minilateralism
9. **Review and Exam Prep** (Module 12): Synthesis, glossary, practice questions
