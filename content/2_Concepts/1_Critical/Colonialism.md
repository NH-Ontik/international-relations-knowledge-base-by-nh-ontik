# Colonialism

## Overview
The practice of domination by one people over another, involving settlement, resource extraction, and political control. Central to [[Postcolonialism]], [[Marxism]], and [[International Development]].

## Key Themes
- Settler colonialism vs extractive colonialism
- Colonial state formation and border drawing
- Economic exploitation and underdevelopment
- Cultural and epistemic violence
- Colonial legacy in contemporary institutions
