## General IR Exam Strategies
1. Define key concepts clearly (theory connects to concept)
2. Compare and contrast: realism vs liberalism, constructivism vs postcolonialism
3. Use case studies for each theory (Cold War, Ukraine, EU, Rwanda)
4. Cite key scholars: Waltz, Wendt, Keohane, Tickner, Said
5. Structure: define → compare → evaluate → apply → conclude

## Common Exam Themes
- [[Anarchy]] — Is it foundational? What does it cause?
- [[Cooperation]] — Why do states cooperate?
- [[Power]] — Hard, soft, structural
- [[Change in IR]] — What changes, what stays the same?
- [[Methodology]] — How do we study IR?
